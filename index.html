Grace Werner

Superintelligence is within reach.

I am a policy associate and the interim Chief of Staff at the Center for International Security and AI (CISAI). I principally supports domestic and international AI governance. Broadly, I believe development of artificial intelligence could produce outcomes ranging from utopian to catastrophic. I believe all sensible approaches to managing these risks and enhancing benefits point toward US leadership and international engagement as the optimal path forward.

Previously, I worked in corporate strategy at Visa; policy and business analysis at O'Donnell Economics and Strategy; and intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaisance at Sandia National Laboratories

M.Sc. in international relations, international political economy, London School of Economics; B.A. in political science, University of New Mexico
